Stay Safe Online

How to Recognize Phishing Scams

  • Check the URL: Ensure the website URL matches the legitimate website address.
  • Look for HTTPS: Secure sites start with HTTPS and display a padlock icon.
  • Be Cautious with Emails: Avoid clicking on links in unsolicited emails or texts.
  • Verify Requests: Contact the organization directly if you receive unexpected requests for sensitive information.
  • Check for Typos: Phishing sites often have spelling errors and unusual formatting.
